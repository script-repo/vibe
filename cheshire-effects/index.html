CHESHIRE VISUALS

This is a creative demo to showcase video effect capabilities and procedural audio, in the style of the 1990s demo scene, IDM electronic music based on tracker (again a 1990s shout-out), an interactive audio effects dashboard, with individual track control and fades. And some MTV-style pop-up video text. I created this in a couple of hours for fun.
